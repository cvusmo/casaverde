## Sensor Requirements

Affordable sensors focused on programmability, compatibility with Orange Pi, and suitability for a greenhouse environment.

## pH Sensor (Soil Probe)
- Requirements: A probe to insert into the soil, affordable, programmable, and capable of transmitting data.
- Recommendation: [AtlasScientific pH Probe #ENV-30-pH](https://atlas-scientific.com/probes/consumer-grade-ph-probe/)
	- Specs:
		- Range 2-13 pH
		- Resolution +/- 0.1 pH
		- Accuracy +/- 0.1 pH
		- Response Time 95% in 4s
		- Temperature Range 1 - 60C
		- Max depth 35m
		- Life Expectancy 12-18 months
		- mounts in 1/2" pvc pipe
		- Price: $50
## Temperature Probe
- Requirements: A probe to measure ambient air, affordable, programmable, and capable of tx/rx data.
- Recommendation [AtlasScientific SMA PT-1000](https://atlas-scientific.com/kits/sma-temperature-kit/)
	- Specs:
		- Range -126 C to 1254 C
		- Resolution 0.001
		- Accuracy +/- 0.1 + 0.0017 x C
		- Response Time 1 reading per second
		- Data protocol UART & I2C
		- Default I2C Address 102 (0x66)
		- Operating voltage 3.3V to 5.5V
		- Data format ASCII
		- Onboard Data Logger 50 Readings
		- $66
## Humidity Probe
- Requirements: A probe to measure humidity in the greenhouse, water-proof, durable, programmable, and capable of tx/rx data.
- Recommendation [AtlasScientific EZO-HUM #EZO-HUM-P-S](https://atlas-scientific.com/probes/humidity-probe/)
	- Specs:
		- Range 0 to 100% RH
		- Reads Relative Humidity, Dew Point, Air Temperature
		- Response time 1 reading per second (UART) or 1 reading per 300ms (I2C mode)
		- Accuracy +/- 2%
		- Data protocol UART & I2C
		- Default I2C address 111 (0x6F)
		- Operating voltage 3.3V - 5V
		- IP rating IP67 (waterproof up to 1m)
		- life expectancy 10 years
		- $105
