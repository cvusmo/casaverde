Casaverde is a gardening app to help manage your garden. It's lightweight, and simple to install. It uses two Orange Pi 4 LTS' with a server. 

# Concept of Design
Wireless monitoring and control of multiple sensors and valves in a greenhouse. Sensors include humidity, temperature, moisture, pH, solar, and valves for efficient water-management.
